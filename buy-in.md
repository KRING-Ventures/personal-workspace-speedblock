# Personal Workspace — Why It's Worth It

Most of your week disappears into email, hunting for files, and scheduling. Personal Workspace gives you your own AI agent that takes that off your plate — so your day is faster and calmer.

## The drain it removes
- **~28%** of your week — email
- **~1.8 hrs** a day — searching for information
- **~10 hrs** a month — calendar & meetings

## What you get
- **A clean inbox.** Draft replies waiting each morning — you review and send.
- **One-question search.** Find anything across mail, files, and calendar by just asking.
- **Nothing slips.** It tracks your follow-ups and what you're owed.
- **Hours back, every week.**

## What it asks of you
Almost nothing. Setup takes about **5 business days** — from activation to your first conversation — and KRING does the work. You confirm a few accounts, name your agent, and have one 15-minute first conversation.

## What it costs to run
A baseline monthly cost **per person**, covering the apps your agent runs on plus hosting. You own the app subscriptions directly; hosting is billed by KRING.

| Per person / month | Approx. |
|---|---|
| Google Workspace (Business Starter) | ~€6 |
| Slack (Pro) | ~€8 |
| ChatGPT (Business Team) | ~€25 |
| Agent hosting & runtime | confirmed with you by KRING |

> These are public-list app prices and vary by plan and currency. KRING confirms your all-in per-seat figure — apps plus hosting — before you commit.
