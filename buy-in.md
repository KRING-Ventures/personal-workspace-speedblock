# Personal Workspace — Why It's Worth It

Most of your week disappears into email, hunting for files, and scheduling. Personal Workspace gives you your own AI agent that takes that off your plate — so your day is faster and calmer.

## The drain it removes
- **~28%** of your week — email
- **~1.8 hrs** a day — searching for information
- **~10 hrs** a month — calendar & meetings

## What you get
- **A clean inbox.** Draft replies waiting each morning — you review and send.
- **One-question search.** Find anything across mail, files, and calendar by just asking.
- **Nothing slips.** It tracks your follow-ups and what you're owed.
- **Hours back, every week.**

## What it asks of you
Almost nothing. Setup takes ~5 days and KRING does the work — you confirm a few accounts, name your agent, and have one 15-minute first conversation.
