# Personal Workspace — Why It's Worth It

The case for Personal Workspace, in plain terms: the time it gives back, what each person gets, and what it means for the venture as a whole.

> Personal Workspace is a standardised, AI-native way of working. Every person gets their own AI agent that handles the busywork — email, calendar, finding things, follow-ups — so the day is faster, calmer, and more fun.

---

## The problem it solves

Three big time drains, the same across almost every knowledge worker:

| Where the time goes | How much |
|---|---|
| **Email** — sorting, prioritising, replying, following up | ~28% of the work week |
| **Searching for information** — files spread across Drive, mail, chat | ~1.8 hours a day |
| **Calendar & meetings** — bookings, prep, scheduling | ~10 hours a month |

That's most of a work week spent on things that don't move anything forward. Personal Workspace takes the weight of that off each person.

---

## What you get

Your own AI agent that works alongside you every day:

- **An inbox that sorts itself** — an overview every morning, not 121 unread.
- **Draft replies in your own tone** — no blank screen, just review and send.
- **Find anything with one question** — across email, files, and calendar, without app-hopping.
- **Nothing falls through** — it tracks what you owe people and what you're waiting on.
- **Hours won back, every week** — the busywork happens in the background.
- **A workday that's faster — and more fun.**

You stay in control. The agent drafts and organises freely, but anything that leaves your account or affects other people gets your OK first.

---

## What it means for the venture

Personal Workspace isn't just a tool per person — it's a shared way of working:

- **One setup for everyone** — the whole team works the same way, from the same data.
- **Shared value across the team** — knowledge and momentum compound instead of living in silos.
- **A tech stack built for humans *and* AI** — the right tools, wired to work together.
- **Hours back across the team** — multiplied by every person using it.
- **A living feedback loop** — the system keeps surfacing impact, new ideas, and how people feel about it.

---

## How it rolls out

**Think big, start small, scale fast.** Personal Workspace deploys per venture in about **5 business days**, with almost no effort asked of the team: you confirm a few accounts and name your agents, KRING sets everything up behind the scenes, and each person meets their agent in a ~15-minute first conversation.

Full step-by-step in `activation.md` (the venture view) and `onboarding.md` (your first conversation with your agent).
