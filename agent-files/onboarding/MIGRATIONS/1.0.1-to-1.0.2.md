# 1.0.1 → 1.0.2 — Boot-budget restructure

*Guidance, not a script. Read it, then apply what fits your state.*

## Why this matters to you

The files OpenClaw loads every session (`AGENTS.md`, `SOUL.md`, `IDENTITY.md`, `USER.md`, `TOOLS.md`, `MEMORY.md`, `HEARTBEAT.md`) share a ~60k-character budget. The framework's `AGENTS.md` had grown past it, so the runtime was truncating the bundle — and `MEMORY.md`, loaded last, was the first thing dropped. If your long-term memory has felt like it wasn't reaching you, this is why. Run `openclaw doctor` to see your own truncation status.

## What changed in the framework

These are framework files, so **pulling the latest framework is enough to get the new versions** — you don't rewrite them by hand:

- `AGENTS.md` is ~55% smaller. Same rules, fewer words; detailed how-tos now live in `runbooks/` and load on demand.
- The old `## Procedures` (verify-before-stating / clear-and-complete / do-first) are now `## How you answer` — three plain principles. **Stop emitting a "Proof" / source-ref on every factual claim.** The bar is: don't state a guess as fact, and flag real uncertainty. A checked answer needs no citation.
- `KRING.md` is now **read on demand** (when a KRING/org question comes up), not at every boot. Drop it from your routine boot reads.
- `SOUL.md` no longer repeats the permission table — that lives in `AGENTS.md` now.

## The one thing to actually do

**If you edited your own `AGENTS.md` or `SOUL.md`** (for example, you added a personal rule like a channel-reply convention near the top): those edits sit on top of the *old, bloated* structure. After pulling the new framework versions, **re-apply only your personal additions on top of the new lean files** — don't carry the old bulk back in. Keep your addition small and put it in the section it belongs to. If you never edited those files, there's nothing to do — the pull handles it.

## What is NOT touched

Your `MEMORY.md`, `memory/*.md`, `USER.md`, `automations/`, and your history are untouched. This is a framework restructure, not a reset. Set your `STATE_VERSION` to `1.0.2` once you've pulled and reconciled, and confirm `openclaw doctor` no longer reports truncation.
